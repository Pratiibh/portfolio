About Me

I was born in Renton, Washington, and have lived all around the state since. My parents were both born in Punjab, Northern India, and moved here in the 90's to provide a better education and life for their descendants. I attended the University of Washington and stopped halfway through a bachelors in Electrical Engineering because I realized what I truly had a passion for - coding! Because of the computer science classes that are required for Electrical Engineering, I was able to dip my feet in the water in regards to computer software and coding, and although I was horrible at it, I truly loved the feeling of implementing working solutions. Nothing felt better than having my code compile and run and it felt as if I was creating life!
